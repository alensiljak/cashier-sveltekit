# Account Tree

Account Tree shows your accounts organized hierarchically by their colon-separated name segments, each with a running balance.

Balances roll up: a parent account's balance is the sum of its own postings plus all of its descendants'. Tap the arrow next to an account to expand or collapse its children; top-level accounts start expanded. Tap an account's name to open its [Account Transactions](/help/account-transactions) view.

Income, Expenses, and Liabilities account names are color-coded, matching the [Accounts](/help/accounts) list.

By default balances are shown in their original currencies. Use the toolbar menu's **Show balances in base currency** option to convert everything to your default currency instead; toggle it again to switch back to original balances.
