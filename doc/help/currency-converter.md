# Currency Converter

Converts an amount between two currencies using the prices recorded in your ledger.

Enter an **Amount**, then pick the **From** and **To** currencies from the searchable dropdowns — the conversion runs automatically after a short pause. Tap the swap button (between the two dropdowns) to reverse the From and To currencies.

The result appears in a card below, showing the converted amount and currency; tap the copy button on the card to copy the result to the clipboard.

If no price is available to convert between the chosen currencies, an error message is shown in place of the result.

You can open this page from the navigation drawer, or from a [Commodity Detail](/help/commodity-detail) page's toolbar menu, which pre-fills the **From** currency with that commodity.
