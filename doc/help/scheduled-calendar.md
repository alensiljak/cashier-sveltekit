# Calendar

A monthly calendar view of your [Scheduled Transactions](/help/scheduled-transactions), reached from the Scheduled Transactions list's toolbar menu (⋮) → **Calendar**.

Each day with one or more scheduled transactions due (by next due date) is marked with a red dot below the date; today is outlined with a ring. Use the arrows above the calendar to move to the previous or next month.

Tap a day to show the scheduled transactions due that day in a list below the calendar; tap it again to hide the list. Each entry shows the payee, the first line of any remarks, and the amount. Tap an entry to open its scheduled transaction actions, where you can view, edit, post, or skip it.
