# Scheduled Transaction Actions

Tapping a scheduled transaction opens this screen, showing its payee, amount, recurrence (e.g. "Repeats every 1 month" and an optional end date), and remarks, along with four actions:

- **Enter** — opens a Journal Entry editor pre-filled with this occurrence, and advances the schedule to its next due date. Confirm first; the transaction isn't saved to the journal until you save it from the editor.
- **Skip** — advances the schedule to its next due date without creating a journal entry (asks for confirmation first).
- **Edit** — opens the scheduled transaction editor to change its payee, postings, amount, or recurrence.
- **Delete** — permanently removes this scheduled transaction, including its recurrence (asks for confirmation first).
