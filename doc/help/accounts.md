# Accounts

The Accounts page lists every account in your ledger.

Account names are colored by type:

- **Income** — teal
- **Expenses** — red
- **Assets** — default text color
- **Liabilities** — purple

Tap an account to open its [Account Transactions](/help/account-transactions) view.

## Selecting an account

This same list doubles as an account picker. When you tap **Account** on a posting in the [Transaction Editor](/help/transaction-editor) (or on a similar account field elsewhere in the app), you're sent here in selection mode. Tapping an account then returns you to where you started with that account filled in, instead of opening the account's transaction history.

Use the search bar at the top to narrow the list by name while picking.
