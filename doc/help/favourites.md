# Favourites

Favourites are a shortlist of accounts you check often, shown with their current balances.

Tap a favourite to open its [Account Transactions](/help/account-transactions). Accounts that no longer exist in your ledger are shown grayed out.

## Adding a favourite

Tap the **+** button (bottom-right) or use **Add** in the toolbar menu (⋮) — both open the account list in selection mode. Tap an account there to add it to Favourites.

## Toolbar menu (⋮)

- **Add** — same as the **+** button; pick an account to add.
- **Delete** — opens a list of your current favourites with a trash icon on each row; tapping it asks for confirmation, then removes that account from the list. Tap the checkmark button to save.
- **Delete All** — clears the entire Favourites list, after confirmation.
- **Reorder** — drag favourites into a new order, then tap the checkmark button to save.
