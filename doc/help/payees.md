# Payees

The Payees page lists every distinct payee found across your transactions.

## Selecting a payee

This list doubles as a payee picker. When you tap the **Payee** field in the [Transaction Editor](/help/transaction-editor), you're sent here in selection mode. Tapping a payee returns you to the transaction with that payee filled in.

While in selection mode, if your search term doesn't match any existing payee, a checkmark button appears — tap it to use the typed text as a brand-new payee.

Use the search bar at the top to filter the list.
