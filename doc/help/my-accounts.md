# My Accounts

The My Accounts screen shows your accounts organized into groups — Savings, Cash Accounts, Credit Cards, or any grouping you choose. Each group is a card listing its accounts with a running total per currency shown at the bottom.

Tap an account row to open its [Account Transactions](/help/account-transactions).

Tap the settings icon on a group's card header to rename the group, change its card colour, add or remove accounts, reorder accounts within the group, or delete the group entirely.

Use the toolbar menu (⋮) to add a new group or reorder existing groups.
